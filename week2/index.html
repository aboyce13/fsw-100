TRIUMPH TR3! Produced from 1955-1962 Available with a 1,991cc engine with a four speed. Some came with overdrive. Very rare option. The cars where assembled in Coventry, England.

My favorite color would be pearl white, but the car came in many colors. The most sought after was British Racing Green

Today this car is a valuable collectors items. There are many clubs around the US where owners can get together.
